About the Organization

Bridging Innovation & Tradition in the Heart of Europe

Our Mission

We believe in transparency, global cooperation, and the free exchange of talent and ideas. Founded in Zurich, our organization serves as a premier forum for technical and humanitarian discussions, hosting over 50 conferences annually across the Swiss Confederation.

We are committed to creating a secure, constructive, and supportive atmosphere for all participants. Conferences are a part of the legacy of this organization and continue today to be a catalyst for creativity. From the diplomatic hubs of Geneva to the financial centers of Zurich, we are dedicated to developing strategies that support and expand conference activities throughout the world.
